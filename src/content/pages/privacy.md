## Estimate requests

When you submit the contact form, the information you enter is sent directly to FormBackend so Hammer Heating and Air Conditioning can respond to your request. This may include your name, email address, phone number, service selection, and message. Do not include sensitive information in the message field.

FormBackend processes form submissions on behalf of this website. Its own terms and privacy practices also apply to that service.

## Website analytics

This website may use Google Analytics 4 to understand general website traffic and whether visitors use important contact links or successfully submit the contact form. Google Analytics may set cookies and collect information such as device details, approximate location, referring pages, and pages visited.

The site does not enable advertising features or Google Signals through its custom analytics code. Analytics is disabled entirely when no valid Google Analytics measurement ID is configured.

## External links

Links to third-party websites, including Yelp, are governed by those websites’ own privacy policies.

## Questions

For questions about this policy or information sent through the website, contact Hammer Heating and Air Conditioning using the phone number or email address shown on this site.
