The company began with Bill Hammer and remains centered on the kind of service where customers can speak directly with the owner. The work shown on the original Hammer website included new central heating and air-conditioning installations, system change-outs, repairs, remodels, thermostats, wall heaters, and related comfort work.

Hammer offers free in-home estimates, senior discounts, and referrals upon request. For questions about a particular system or project, call Bill or send an estimate request.
